Join Our Lab

Admission

We welcome students passionate about moral psychology, behavioral public administration, and civic education. Whether you come from psychology, public administration, sociology, or philosophy, if you are curious about how people navigate conflict, this is a place to grow.

Programs

Recruitment

Doctoral Students

  • Direction: Public Administration (international doctoral students)
  • Topics: behavioral public administration, public administrative ethics
  • Supervisor: Associate Professor Chuanjun Liu

Academic Master's

  • Direction: Sociology (Social Psychology)
  • Topics: moral psychology, civic education
  • For domestic students; 3-year program

Professional Master's

  • Direction: Applied Psychology (professional master's)
  • Topics: mental conflict, applied behavioral public administration
  • For domestic students

International Students

  • MA: Applied Psychology, Public Administration
  • PhD: Public Administration
  • Language: English / Chinese

How to Apply

Application

Contact the Supervisor

Interested students should email Associate Professor Liu with a brief introduction of your academic background, research interests, and future plans, along with your CV: liuchuanjun@scu.edu.cn

What You'll Get

  • Multidisciplinary training with one-on-one supervision
  • Opportunities to participate in national-level research grants
  • Cross-cultural environment with international students
  • Equal status as a "junior scientist candidate" with full authority over your projects
  • Open science training: pre-registration, fully open data
WeChat Official Account

Learn About Us First: Follow mentalconflict

If you're still deciding whether to join, follow our WeChat Official Account mentalconflict to see what kind of research we do and what academic life looks like in the lab.

We Look Forward to Hearing From You

Whether you want to learn about our research or are ready to apply, email Associate Professor Liu.
