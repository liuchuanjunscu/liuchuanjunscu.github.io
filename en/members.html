Chuanjun Liu

Associate Professor · Doctoral Supervisor

From Renshou, Sichuan. Associate Professor and doctoral supervisor in the Department of Sociology and Psychology, School of Public Administration, Sichuan University. BA in Philosophy and MA in Psychology from Nanjing University; PhD in Social Psychology from Tsinghua University. Joined Sichuan University in August 2020.

Research areas: social psychology, behavioral public administration, and civic education. Published 30+ papers in CSSCI and SSCI journals; authored Behavioral Ethics and Learning to Respect. Leads 10+ research grants including the National Natural Science Foundation of China.

Lab Culture

Culture

The lab upholds a culture of equality, rigor, and openness. Every student is positioned as a junior scientist candidate, enjoying fully equal status with the supervisor and holding the highest authority over their own projects.

Growth Path of Junior Scientists

Training Path

A

Maintain Curiosity & Diligence

Cultivate deep curiosity about everyday phenomena and the rigor to examine them thoroughly.

B

Read Two Types of Literature

General methodology literature plus topic-specific literature to locate the knowledge frontier.

C

Choose a Moderate Knowledge Gap

Find the right-sized gap to push the boundary forward.

D

Follow Academic Norms

From literature review to pre-registration, ethics review, data collection, analysis, and publication.
